## 4. College Athletic Scholarship Opportunities

### Overall Probability

- **<2% of high school athletes** receive ANY NCAA athletic scholarship
- **~6% of high school athletes** compete at NCAA level (D1, D2, D3)
- **~80% of D3 athletes** receive academic/need-based aid (~$20k average)

### 2025-26 Changes (House v. NCAA Settlement)

As of July 1, 2025:
- **All sports become "equivalency" sports** (coaches can split scholarships)
- **Roster limits replace scholarship caps**
- **More scholarships available** in non-revenue sports
- **Example**: Baseball expands from 11.7 scholarships to potential 34-player roster
- **Effect**: Increased opportunities, but fewer full-ride scholarships overall

## 11. App Framework Alignment with Reality

### How the App Uses This Data

1. **Cost Ranges**: Based on real 2024 averages by sport
2. **Participation Rates**: Used to calculate "regional competition" scores
3. **Physical Demands**: Scored 1-5 based on research data
4. **College Opportunities**: Based on NCAA scholarship data (updated for House settlement)
5. **Injury Risk**: Incorporated into physical fitness matching algorithm
6. **Lifespan Value**: Based on adult participation trends
7. **Height Advantage**: Scientifically-backed scoring model
8. **Local Programs**: Pulled from real national/regional databases

### Scoring Algorithm Factors

The app weighs factors based on verified research:
- **Fun Factor** (30%): Sport popularity + participation trends
- **Skill Development** (25%): Physical demands + coaching availability
- **Competition Level** (20%): Regional participation rates
- **Physical Fit** (15%): Height advantage + physical demands
- **Affordability** (10%): Regional cost data
